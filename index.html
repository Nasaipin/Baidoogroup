About Baidoo Hostel

Providing quality accommodation for students since 2020

Your Home Away From Home

Hostel Haven has been providing comfortable and affordable accommodation to students for over 4 years. Our mission is to create a supportive environment that fosters both academic success and personal growth.

We understand the challenges of student life, which is why we've designed our facilities to meet all your needs - from quiet study areas to social spaces where you can connect with fellow students.

Our dedicated staff is available 24/7 to ensure your safety and comfort, making Baidoo Hostel the perfect choice for your academic journey.

Academic Excellence Scholarship

For students maintaining a GPA of 3.0 or above and demonstrate the need for sponsorship get up to at least 50% of accommodation costs covered.

Sports Scholarship

For students representing Baidoo FC in sports. Covers up to 50% of accommodation costs.
